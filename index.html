Fears & Phobias

Fear is a normal feeling when something is dangerous. It helps us stay safe. A phobia is a very strong fear of a thing or situation, even when it is not really dangerous. With a phobia, people often feel anxiety and try to avoid the thing. Phobias can be treated. Step-by-step practice and talking therapies can help.
